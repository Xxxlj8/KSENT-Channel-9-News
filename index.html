BDC Member in Blue Runs Up From Alley, Executes Demon Rival in Black Coupe

By KSENT Staff | March 3, 2025

A brutal gang execution took place last night on **47th & Elm Street**, where a **BDC gang member—dressed in all blue with a blue bandana wrapped around his head**—ambushed a rival **Demon member** sitting in a **black coupe**, shooting him at close range.

Security footage confirms that the gunman **emerged from an alley at the 10:00 position relative to the car**, approaching with a gun drawn. Within seconds, he fired multiple shots through the **driver’s side window**, delivering a lethal hit before escaping back into the alley.

Silent Approach, Deadly Execution

The victim, identified as **24-year-old Raymond “Ray Ray” Mitchell**, was reportedly sitting in his vehicle, scrolling through his phone, when he was ambushed. Witnesses say the shooter, **clad entirely in blue, with a blue bandana on his head**, moved with **purpose and precision**.

**"The dude had a blue bandana tight on his head, clear as day,"** said a gas station employee who witnessed the attack. **"He came from the alley, no hesitation. Walked straight up, raised his gun, and let off."**

After firing, the suspect sprinted **back into the same alley** he emerged from, **disappearing within seconds.**

Forensic Findings at the Scene

Detectives recovered **five .40 caliber shell casings** near the car, indicating controlled shots. Analysis of the car's shattered window suggests that the bullets entered at a **downward angle**, confirming the gunman fired at close range.

**"The shooter knew exactly where to aim,"** said forensic analyst Lisa Ramirez. **"Four shots hit the chest, one to the neck—this was an execution, not a warning."**

Police also found a **loaded 9mm pistol** inside the victim’s car, but Mitchell **never had time to react or defend himself**.

Surveillance Footage & Suspect Search

Authorities are reviewing **multiple security cameras** from the area. The footage clearly shows the shooter’s attire:

  • All-blue outfit, **matching BDC colors**
  • **Blue bandana tied tightly on his head**
  • Black gloves, dark sneakers

**"The suspect’s body language suggests he was trained or experienced,"** said Detective James Carter. **"He approached with confidence, fired quickly, and escaped without hesitation."**

Escalating Tensions & Retaliation Fears

This execution follows weeks of **increasing violence** between the **BDC** and **Demon factions**. Just last month, a **BDC associate was killed in a drive-by on 38th Street**, fueling speculation that **Mitchell’s death was a direct retaliation.**

With both gangs **on high alert**, police are bracing for a fresh wave of **bloodshed**.

Community Reacts: "This War Has to Stop"

Residents, already weary of gang violence, expressed frustration over the **continued cycle of killings.**

**"These kids are dying over colors,"** said an elderly woman who has lived in the area for 30 years. **"We’re losing a whole generation to this nonsense."**

Local activists are organizing a **candlelight vigil** for Mitchell, though tensions remain high, with fears that the vigil itself could become a **target for retaliation.**

Police Urge Witnesses to Come Forward

Law enforcement officials are urging anyone with information to contact them. The **KSENT Crime Hotline** has already received **several anonymous tips**, but police need **stronger leads.**

Anyone with relevant details is urged to call **KSENT Crime Hotline** at **(555) 0199.**

As gang violence intensifies, authorities have increased patrols in known hot zones, fearing this latest hit may **only be the beginning.**
